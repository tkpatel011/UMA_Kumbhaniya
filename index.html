Our Story

Crafted with Heart,
Served with Soul

Since 2022
3+ Years of Taste

UMA Kumbhaniya is more than a restaurant — it's a celebration of authentic Gujarati and Indian flavours passed down through generations.

Nestled in the heart of the community, we bring you the warmth of home-cooked food with the finesse of professional cooking. Every dish is crafted from fresh, locally-sourced ingredients, ensuring that every meal is a memorable experience.
